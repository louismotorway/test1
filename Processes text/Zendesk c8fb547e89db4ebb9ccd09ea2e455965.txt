Zendesk


	1.	Open zendesk admin portal https://motorway.zendesk.com/admin/people/team/agents
	2.	search for the user and open their profile
	3.	Click ‘Suspend access’￼ 
	4.	If the user has tickets assigned to them contact their line manager to get them reassigned
	5.	Change the users account type to ‘end-user’￼ 
	6.	Open the ‘views’ tab https://motorway.zendesk.com/admin/workspaces/agent-workspace/views?access=shared&active=true&page=1&per_page=100&sort_by=position
	7.	search for the users view, click the elipses and then ‘deactivate view’￼
