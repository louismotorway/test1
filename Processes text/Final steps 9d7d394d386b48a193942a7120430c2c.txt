Final steps
Send Onelogin invites
the last day of the week before someone joins do the following:
	1.	Open Onelogin https://motorway.onelogin.com/users
	2.	search for the user and open their profile
	3.	click ‘More actions’ and ‘send invitation’￼ 
	4.	Open BambooHR https://motorway.bamboohr.com/
	5.	search for the user and open their profile
	6.	copy the users personal email address and use this to send the invite (the user would not have started yet so has no access to corporate email)￼ 
	7.	Open the ticket in HaloITSM, click ‘Email user’, click ‘insert canned text’ and select ‘Onelogin Invitation & First day guide’￼ 
	8.	Change the ‘To’ address to the users personal email and update the {{FIRST NAME}} etc fields, changing their name and email address and your name when signing off the email, and press send.
