Fresh Sales
FreshSales is a platform used in a small quantity by the Sales department teams (Account Managers and Dealer Experience) as opposed to using Zendesk. Please follow the steps below to setup access: 

	1.	log into https://motorway.freshsales.io/settings/users/view/active
	2.	Select Add user
	3.	Set the user’s team to reflect their team
	4.	Set the user’s Pipeline to reflect their team
	5.	Set the user’s manager to their line manager 
	6.	Set the user’s Job title 
	7.	Set the User’s role to user ￼
